### 3.2 Recruiting Strategies

**Channels:**

1. **Internal Referrals** (Primary - lowest cost, fastest)
   - $5,000 referral bonus per successful hire
   - Email all employees with job descriptions
   - Track referrals in spreadsheet
   - Target: 50% of hires via referrals

2. **Direct Sourcing** (LinkedIn, GitHub)
   - Dedicated sourcer (internal or agency)
   - 20+ candidates per position
   - Personalized outreach
   - Target: 30% of hires

3. **Job Boards** (AngelList, Hacker News, Indeed)
   - Post on 5+ platforms
   - Sponsored listings
   - SEO-optimized descriptions
   - Target: 15% of hires

4. **Technical Recruiters** (Backup)
   - 2-3 agency partnerships
   - Contingency fee (20-25%)
   - Use for hard-to-fill positions
   - Target: 5% of hires (cost-effective)

5. **University Partnerships** (Junior roles)
   - Target top CS programs
   - Internship-to-hire pipeline
   - Campus recruiting events
   - Target: Junior engineers only

### 7.1 Weekly Capacity Calculation

```
Total Capacity (Story Points) = Team Size Ã— Days per Sprint Ã— Points per Day

Where:
- Team Size = Number of engineers
- Days per Sprint = 10 (2-week sprint)
- Points per Day = 0.7 (accounting for meetings, context switching)

Example:
12 engineers Ã— 10 days Ã— 0.7 = 84 theoretical points
Adjusted for meetings/overhead (20%) = 67 available points
Sprint commitment = 67 Ã— 0.85 (buffer) = ~57 points
```

## 18. CONTINGENCY STAFFING

### 18.1 If Hiring Delays Occur

**Mitigation Options:**

**Option 1: Contractors**
- Engage contractor for 30-60 days
- Cost: Higher hourly rate
- Benefit: Immediate availability

**Option 2: Extend Timeline**
- Delay phase start by 1-2 weeks
- Cost: Minimal
- Benefit: Wait for right hire

**Option 3: Internal Reallocation**
- Borrow from other teams
- Cost: Opportunity cost
- Benefit: Cultural fit, ramp-up faster

**Option 4: Scope Adjustment**
- Defer non-critical features
- Cost: Feature delay
- Benefit: Maintain quality

### 18.2 Rapid Scaling Procedures

**If need to scale quickly (>2 people in 2 weeks):**

1. Activate all recruiting channels simultaneously
2. Offer signing bonuses ($5K-$10K)
3. Expedite interview process (3 days vs 2 weeks)
4. Consider acqui-hire opportunities
5. Engage multiple recruiting agencies
6. Relax some nice-to-have requirements

- Interview candidates
- Update team structure
- Adjust sprint commitments
