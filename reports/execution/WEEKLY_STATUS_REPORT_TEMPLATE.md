1. **Be Concise:** Executives want key information quickly. Use bullet points and visuals.

2. **Be Honest:** Report both good and bad news. Hiding problems makes them worse.

3. **Be Specific:** Use data and metrics. Avoid vague statements like "things are going well."

4. **Be Forward-Looking:** Balance reporting on the past with planning for the future.

5. **Be Actionable:** Every issue should have a clear owner and action plan.
