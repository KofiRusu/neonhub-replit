## 3. DISTRIBUTION PROCEDURES

### 3.1 Preparation Phase (Before Distribution)

**Checklist:**
- [ ] All 11 documents finalized and approved by CEO
- [ ] Documents converted to PDF (read-only format)
- [ ] Distribution list verified and updated
- [ ] Email templates customized per stakeholder
- [ ] Read receipt tracking enabled
- [ ] Distribution spreadsheet prepared
- [ ] Escalation contacts notified of pending distribution

**Responsible:** Chief of Staff  
**Timeline:** 1 hour before distribution

### 3.2 Distribution Wave Strategy

#### Wave 1: Executive Leadership (T+0)
**Recipients:** CEO, CFO, CTO, General Counsel  
**Method:** Hand-delivered + Email with read receipt  
**Expected Response:** Immediate acknowledgment

#### Wave 2: Extended Leadership (T+30 min)
**Recipients:** CMO, VP Engineering, VP Product, Board Chair  
**Method:** Email with read receipt + SMS notification  
**Expected Response:** Within 2 hours

#### Wave 3: Key Stakeholders (T+1 hour)
**Recipients:** Board members, Lead Investors, VP Customer Success  
**Method:** Email with read receipt  
**Expected Response:** Within 4 hours

#### Wave 4: Operational Teams (T+2 hours)
**Recipients:** Directors, Managers, Team Leads  
**Method:** Email via distribution list  
**Expected Response:** Within 8 hours

### 3.3 Distribution Execution Steps

1. **Send Executive Summary Email** (5 min)
   - High-priority flag enabled
   - Subject: "URGENT: NeonHub Agent Crisis Remediation - Executive Action Required"
   - Body: Executive summary + next steps
   - Attachments: All relevant documents per routing matrix

2. **Enable Read Receipt Tracking** (Immediate)
   - Configure email client for delivery + read receipts
   - Document tracking spreadsheet prepared
   - Auto-alerts configured for non-opens after 1 hour

3. **Follow-up Protocols**
   - T+1 hour: SMS reminder if no acknowledgment
   - T+2 hours: Phone call if no acknowledgment
   - T+3 hours: Escalate to backup contact
   - T+4 hours: Executive escalation

## 6. 4-HOUR ACKNOWLEDGMENT PROTOCOL

### 6.1 Acknowledgment Requirements

**What Counts as Acknowledgment:**
- Email reply with "ACKNOWLEDGED" + name
- Phone confirmation documented in tracking sheet
- In-person confirmation documented
- Digital signature on approval forms (executives only)

**What Does NOT Count:**
- Email opened but no reply
- Out-of-office auto-reply
- Third-party acknowledgment (unless authorized backup)

### 6.2 Escalation Workflow

```
DISTRIBUTION (T+0)
    ↓
NO RESPONSE AFTER 1 HOUR
    ↓
ESCALATION LEVEL 1: SMS Reminder
    ↓ (if no response)
NO RESPONSE AFTER 2 HOURS
    ↓
ESCALATION LEVEL 2: Phone Call
    ↓ (if no response)
NO RESPONSE AFTER 3 HOURS
    ↓
ESCALATION LEVEL 3: Backup Contact
    ↓ (if no response)
NO RESPONSE AFTER 4 HOURS
    ↓
ESCALATION LEVEL 4: CEO Notification + Emergency Protocol
```

### 6.3 Emergency Protocol (4+ Hours No Response)

**If P0 Stakeholder Does Not Acknowledge Within 4 Hours:**

1. **CEO Non-Response:**
   - Notify Board Chair immediately
   - Activate Business Continuity Plan
   - Chief of Staff assumes temporary authority

2. **CFO Non-Response:**
   - Escalate to CEO
   - VP Finance authorized for urgent financial decisions
   - Board Chair notified

3. **CTO Non-Response:**
   - Escalate to CEO
   - VP Engineering assumes technical authority
   - Immediate status check on CTO availability

4. **General Counsel Non-Response:**
   - Escalate to CEO
   - Engage outside legal counsel
   - Senior Legal Counsel authorized for urgent decisions

## 8. DOCUMENT SECURITY & ACCESS CONTROL

### 8.1 Security Measures

**Document Protection:**
- All documents distributed as password-protected PDFs
- Password shared via separate channel (SMS/phone)
- Watermarking with recipient name + date
- Restricted printing and forwarding
- Confidential classification applied

**Password Protocol:**
```
Format: NH-Crisis-[Month][Day]
Example: NH-Crisis-0115
Rotation: Daily for first week
```

### 8.2 Access Control

**Distribution Channels:**
- Primary: Company email (encrypted)
- Backup: Secure file sharing (Dropbox Business, Box, etc.)
- Emergency: Hand-delivery of printed copies (CEO/CFO/CTO only)

**Retention Policy:**
- Emails: Retain indefinitely in legal hold
- Tracking data: Retain for 7 years
- Acknowledgments: Retain permanently
